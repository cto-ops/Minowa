Minowa, Inc. designs and builds software for clinicians, patients, and the teams that support them, with a focus on mobile-first experiences.

About

Minowa, Inc. is a privately held software company. We develop mobile applications and supporting services primarily in the healthcare space, along with selected work in adjacent domains where careful engineering and thoughtful product design matter.

Our work emphasizes clarity, privacy, and durability — software that holds up in the hands of real people doing real work.

What we do

We build iOS and cross-platform mobile applications, the backend services that support them, and the operational tooling that keeps them running. Individual product listings are available on the App Store under the Minowa, Inc. developer name.
